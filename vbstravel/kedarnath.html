  • Day 1

    Haridwar Arrival

    Pick Up from Haridwar railway station. Proceed towards Rishikesh. Visit Gangaghat and Laxman Zhula. Later enjoy river rafting (At own cost). Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

  • Day 2

    Rishikesh to Guptkashi

    Today after breakfast proceed towards Guptkashi. Enroute visit to Devprayag and Rudraprayag Sangam. Overnight stay in Guptkashi.

  • Day 3

    Guptkashi – Sonprayag/Sitapur - Kedarnath

    Early morning check-out from the hotel and drive to Sonprayag/Sitapur From here, we will start a 17 km trek to Kedarnath. Either by walk, Doli or by horse at own cost. In the evening visit Shankaracharya Samadhi behind the temple and take part in the evening Aarti. Overnight stay in Kedarnath 

  • Day 4

    Kedarnath

    Later drive to Chopta. Check-in to Camps. Dinner & Stay at Chopta camps. Overnight stay in Chopta.

  • Day 5

    Chopta to Badrinath

    After Darshan, go sightseeing in Mana Village (the last village before the Tibetan border). Visit Vyas Gufa, Ganesh Gufa, Charan Paduka, Bhim pul and the "Mukh" of the Saraswati river. Evening return to Hotel Dinner & Overnight in Badrinath.

  • Day 6

    Badrinath to Haridwar/Rishikesh

    After breakfast proceed towards Haridwar. Drop at hotel in Haridwar or Rishikesh.
