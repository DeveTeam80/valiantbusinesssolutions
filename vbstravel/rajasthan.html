  • Day 1

    Transfer to the hotel and then visit Birla Mandir, Sanganeri Printing Showroom, and Chokhi Dhani to experience Rajasthani Village tradition

  • Day 2

    Jaipur City Tour

    Proceed to get photos clicked at Hawa Mahal followed by a visit to Amer Fort, Jal Mahal, City Palace. Birla Mandir and Jantar Mantar.

  • Day 3

    Jaipur to Pushkar/Ajmer

    Start your journey towards Ajmer and visit Moinuddin Chishti Dargah, Pushkar Tirth, and Brahma Mandir.

  • Day 4

    Pushkar/Ajmer to Udaipur

    Proceed to experience the beauty of Udaipur and visit Chittorgarh Fort-Rana Kumbha, Mesra Mandir, Vijay Stambha, and Padmini's Palace.

  • Day 5

    Udaipur City Tour

    Witness the city of Lakes and visit Maharana Pratap Smarak - Mot Magri and Saheliyon ki Bari - Garden of the Maids of Honour' A last visit to the City Palace, unique Crystal Gallery followed by light and sound show.

  • Day 6

    Departure from Udaipur

    Transfer to the Airport/Railway Station to board your mode of transport.
