  • Day 1

    Arrive In Dubai

    Today arrive in Dubai city. Check in at the hotel. Later in the evening we will embark on the Marina Dhow Dinner Cruise – A traditional wood sailing vessel which sails from the Heritage village. Relax and experience the enchanted atmosphere of the city by night. Overnight in Dubai. (Dinner)

  • Day 2

    Dubai City Tour, Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall

    Today we explore the amazing city of Dubai. City tour includes a walk around the Jumeirah Beach, and drive through famous Sheikh Zayed Road. Take a photo stop at Atlantis Hotel – the first resort built on the artificial island The Palm. Later we shall visit the tallest building in the world – Burj Khalifa, from here we will get amazing view of this city from the 124th floor. Overnight in Dubai. (Breakfast)

  • Day 3

    Dessert Safari with Bbq Dinner

    Today morning is free for own activities later enjoy thrilling Desert Safari by four wheeled drive Land Cruiser through Sand dunes followed by amazing Belly Dance show along with dinner. Overnight in Dubai. (Breakfast & Dinner in Desert).

  • Day 4

    Abu Dhabi City Tour, Sheikh Zayed Mosque, Ferrari World

    After breakfast proceed to Abu Dhabi for guided city tour visit the world’s largest and famous Sheikh Zayed Mosque. Later we visit Ferrari World. Overnight in Dubai. (Breakfast)

  • Day 5

    Departure To Hometown

    Today check out from hotel and proceed towards Airport to board return flight. (Breakfast)
