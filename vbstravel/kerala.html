  • Day 1

    Kochi-Munnar

    Arrive at Kochi airport/railway station, report to coach proceeds for Munnar. En Route Visit Cheeyapara Waterfall & Valara Waterfall (subject to operation), Arrive in Munnar in the evening. Overnight stay in Munnar.

  • Day 2

    Munnar

    After Breakfast Proceed to local Sightseeing Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam near Sun Moon Valley and enjoy spectacular view of tea gardens. You can buy flavored tea packs at Tata tea Outlet. Later visit Echo Point. Overnight stay in Munnar.

  • Day 3

    Munnar - Thekkady

    After Breakfast Proceed towards Thekkady, Today we visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake (Subject to Operation), Free time for shopping at Kumily Spice Village. Evening free for leisure. Overnight saty in Thekkady

  • Day 4

    Thekkady – Alleppey

    After Breakfast Proceed to Alleppey & Enjoy a Beautiful Houseboat stay and a Backwater Ride. Overnight stay in Alleppey.

  • Day 5

    Alleppey – Kochi

    After Breakfast Proceed towards Kochi. Drop at Kochi airport/railway station.
