  • Day 1

    Bali

    Arrival at Bali international airport. Transfer to hotel. Complete the check in formalities. Rest of the day is free for own activities.

  • Day 2

    Bali

    Full day Kintamani Volcano TourPick-up from your hotel: Your tour guide and driver will pick you up from your hotel early in the morning, usually around 8:00 am. Batubulan Village: Your first stop will be at Batubulan Village, where you'll witness a traditional Balinese Barong dance performance. Celuk Village: You'll then visit Celuk Village, known for its gold and silver jewelry making. You'll have the opportunity to watch skilled craftsmen at work and purchase some souvenirs. Tegalalang Rice Terrace: Next, you'll visit the Tegalalang Rice Terrace, one of the most beautiful and picturesque landscapes in Bali. You can take a walk through the rice paddies and take in the stunning scenery. Kintamani Volcano: The highlight of the tour is a visit to the Kintamani Volcano, which is an active volcano surrounded by a beautiful caldera lake. You'll have lunch with a stunning view of the volcano and the lake. Tirta Empul Temple: After lunch, you'll visit the Tirta Empul Temple, a holy spring temple where Balinese Hindus go for purification rituals. Coffee Plantation: Your last stop will be at a coffee plantation, where you can sample some of Bali's famous Luwak coffee and learn about the coffee-making process.

  • Day 3

    Bali

    Benoa Beach with Uluwatu temple. Benoa Beach: Your first stop will be at Benoa Beach, which is located in the southern part of Bali. You can enjoy various water sports activities such as jet skiing, parasailing, and banana boat rides. You can also relax on the beach, swim, and soak up the sun. Lunch: After enjoying the beach activities, you'll have lunch at a nearby restaurant. Uluwatu Temple: After lunch, you'll head to Uluwatu Temple, which is located on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean. This temple is famous for its stunning sunset views and traditional Balinese architecture. You'll have the opportunity to explore the temple complex and watch a Kecak dance performance, a traditional Balinese dance that tells the story of the Ramayana epic. Return to your hotel: After watching the sunset and the Kecak dance.

  • Day 4

    Bali

    Nusa Penida Tour Ferry to Nusa Penida: You'll take a ferry from the harbor to Nusa Penida Island. The ferry ride usually takes around 45 minutes to an hour. Angel's Billabong: Your first stop will be at Angel's Billabong, a natural pool with crystal clear water located in a rocky shore. You can take a dip in the pool and enjoy the stunning views. Broken Beach: Next, you'll visit Broken Beach, a beautiful natural arch with a clear blue lagoon. This is a popular spot for photos. Kelingking Beach: The highlight of the tour is a visit to Kelingking Beach, which is known for its stunning views and white sand beach. The beach is located at the bottom of a cliff, and you can hike down to the beach if you're up for the adventure. Lunch: After a morning of exploring, you'll have lunch at a local restaurant. Crystal Bay: In the afternoon, you'll visit Crystal Bay, a beautiful beach with crystal clear water, perfect for swimming and Snorkelling. Return to Bali: In the late afternoon, you'll take the ferry back to Bali.
