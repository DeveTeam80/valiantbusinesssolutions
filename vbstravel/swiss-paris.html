  • Day 1

    Arrive

    Arrive at Charles de Gaulle airport. Transfer to Hotel. Later enjoy some of the history of France and the national monuments, then a cruise on the Seine and a visit to the Eiffel Tower. Overnight stay in Paris. (Dinner)

  • Day 2

    Paris

    Today after breakfast visit to the famous Louvre Museum. Your priority access ticket will allow you to follow your guide straight into the world's largest museum. The perfect opportunity to learn about the museum's huge collection and its greatest works of art. Rest of the day is free for leisure. Overnight stay in Paris.

  • Day 3

    Paris

    Today enjoy an exciting trip to Disneyland. Get charmed by Cinderella's castle. Take an exciting roller coaster ride down the Thunder Mountain. Visit Fantasy land and meet Alice in Wonderland, Pinocchio, Peter Pan and Snow White. Get thrilled by the legends of Aladdin and Indiana Jones at Adventure Ride a space shuttle to the moon at Discovery land. Overnight stay in Paris.

  • Day 4

    Paris to Lucern

    Today board train to from Paris to Zurich and later from Zurich to Lucern. Upon arrival in Lucern check in at the hotel. Overnight stay in Lucern.

  • Day 5

    Mt Titlis

    Today visit Mt Titlis enjoy World’s first revolving cable car offering you a magical 360 degrees panoramic view. Amazing Attractions: The Fun Lift, Ice flyer Chair lift, The Cliff Walk - Europe’s highest suspension bridge, The Glacier Grotto and the Glacier Park. Overnight stay in Lucern.

  • Day 6

    Mt Jungfraujoch

    Day visit to Mt. Jungfraujoch: Europe's highest altitude Railway station at 3454 metres. Amazing Attractions: Ice Palace, Ski & Snowboard Park, the Sphinx observation terrace and the "Top of Europe" Glacier Restaurant Famous activities: Snow Disk Run, Tyrolienne Jungfraujoch. Overnight stay in Lucern.

  • Day 7

    Lucern to Zurich

    Proceed to Zurich. Enroute visit Rhine Falls at Schaffhausen (Neuhausen) and enjoy Boat ride to the rock for a spectacular view of the falls. Take a photo stop at Zurich Lake. Arrive in Zurich. Overnight stay in Zurich.

  • Day 8

    Departure

    After breakfast drop at Airport to board return flight.
