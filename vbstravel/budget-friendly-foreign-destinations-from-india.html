Budget Friendly Foreign Destinations From India

10 February

For Indian travelers with a wanderlust spirit and a tight budget, exploring

international destinations

doesn't have to be a distant dream. Numerous countries are offering remarkable experiences without draining your wallet. In this guide, we'll take you through 10 of the

cheapest countries to visit from India

, complete with estimated expenses and must-visit attractions, ensuring your next adventure is both memorable and budget-friendly.


Nepal:


As a neighboring country, Nepal is not only easily accessible but also incredibly affordable



Accommodation:


Accommodation in guesthouses or budget hotels starts from ₹500 to ₹1000 per night.

Meals:


Meals at local eateries are budget-friendly, with prices ranging from ₹100 to ₹300 per meal.

Must Visit Places:

Key Attractions: While the place caters to every type of traveler, it is also a location that attracts more adventurers and backpackers. Key attractions include –



  • Bungee jumping in Pokhara.
  • Pashupatinath temple
  • Sagarmatha National Park
  • The ancient town of Pathan.
  • Rafting at BhoteKoshi.

    Meals:

    Must Visit Places:

    • Thailand:


    Renowned for its vibrant culture and stunning landscapes, Thailand offers affordable experiences for Indian travelers



    Accommodation:


    Hostel dorm beds start from ₹300 per night, while budget guesthouses are available from ₹1000 per night.

    Meals:


    Street food is abundant and cheap, with meals costing as little as ₹50 to ₹200

    Must Visit Places:

    The thriving tourism industry in Thailand, one of the cheapest countries to visit from India, owes its revenue to the main tourist attractions that the country hosts. It includes –



  • Railay beach
  • Koh Phi Phi
  • The Grand Palace, Bangkok
  • Sunday Walking Street, Chiang Mai
  • Pai, etc

    • Vietnam:


    Vietnam's rich history and natural beauty beckon budget travelers with affordable



    Accommodation:


    accommodations and delicious street food. Hostel dorm beds start from ₹300 per night, while guesthouses are available from ₹800 per night

    Meals:


    Enjoy Vietnamese cuisine at local eateries for around ₹100 to ₹300 per meal

    Must Visit Places:

    While sightseeing and visiting the marketplace come as obvious attractions, you have the option for a few unique experiences too. Vietnam offers the following by way of tourist attractions –



  • Con Dao islands
  • My Khe beach
  • The Temple of Literature in Hanoi
  • Cao Dai temple.
  • Visit the Bac Ha market.

    • Cambodia:


    Cambodia offers budget travelers a wealth of experiences, from exploring ancient temples to relaxing on pristine beaches



    Accommodation:


    Options include hostels starting from ₹200 per night and guesthouses from ₹800 per night.

    Meals:


    Indulge in Khmer cuisine at local eateries for around ₹100 to ₹300 per meal.

    Must Visit Places:

    Cambodia as a country, holds the heart of its tourists considering its abundance of tourist offerings which are a perfect amalgamation of natural and manmade attractions. Some such attractions include –



  • Preah Vihear
  • Tonle Sap
  • Bokor Hill Station
  • Kratie
  • Koh Ker, etc
