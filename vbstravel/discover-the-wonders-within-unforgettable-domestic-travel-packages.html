Amritsar - The Land of Golden Temples

Begin your adventure from Amritsar to Dalhousie, where the picturesque landscapes of the Dhauladhar Range will leave you in awe. Explore the hidden gems of Khajjiar and Chamba before returning to Dalhousie. Alternatively, immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance of Dharamshala, the abode of the Dalai Lama, and return to Amritsar with heartwarming memories.

Delhi - The Historical and Modern Marvels

Embark on a historical voyage in Delhi, where ancient monuments coexist with modern wonders. Take a captivating Delhi City Tour before venturing to the sacred cities of Vrindavan and Mathura, leading to the iconic Taj Mahal in Agra. Continue your journey through the historical city of Fatehpur Sikri, and finally, immerse yourself in the royal culture of Jaipur on a majestic city tour.

Kashmir - A Paradise on Earth

Experience the mesmerizing valleys of Kashmir on day excursions from Srinagar. Delight in the breathtaking beauty of Sonmarg and Doodhpathri before exploring the enchanting meadows of Gulmarg. Discover the picturesque landscapes of Pahalgam before concluding your Kashmir adventure with a tranquil houseboat stay in Srinagar.

Rajasthan - The Land of Royalty

Unravel the royal heritage of Jaipur on a captivating city tour before venturing to the sacred cities of Pushkar and Ajmer. Delight in the romantic charm of Udaipur, known for its shimmering lakes and palaces.

Maharashtra - A Tapestry of Diversity

Welcome to Maharashtra, a state that weaves together a beautiful tapestry of diverse experiences. From the bustling metropolis of Mumbai to the serene hills of Lonavala and Khandala, and the ancient caves of Ajanta and Ellora, Maharashtra offers a plethora of attractions. Explore the historic forts of Raigad and Sinhagad, and witness the religious fervor at Shirdi and Nashik. For nature enthusiasts, the verdant beauty of Mahabaleshwar and the pristine beaches of Alibaug beckon. Maharashtra's rich culture and vibrant festivals, such as Ganesh Chaturthi, add a unique charm to your journey, promising an unforgettable experience.

GOA - Where Fun Meets Relaxation

Welcome to the land of sun, sand, and sea - Goa! Renowned for its laid-back vibe and vibrant nightlife, Goa promises an unforgettable vacation. Embrace the sun-kissed beaches of Calangute, Baga, and Anjuna, where you can indulge in water sports and beach parties. Explore the Portuguese heritage in the charming streets of Old Goa, featuring historical churches and colonial architecture. For a serene escape, head to South Goa, where you'll find tranquil beaches like Palolem and Agonda. Goa's unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation ensures a memorable experience for every traveler.

Kerala - God's Own Country

Welcome to the enchanting land of Kerala, where nature's beauty is at its finest. Explore the serene backwaters of Alleppey on a traditional houseboat, immersing yourself in the tranquility of the surroundings. Discover the mist-covered hills of Munnar, known for its sprawling tea estates and picturesque landscapes. Witness the wildlife wonders at Periyar National Park, where you can spot elephants and other exotic species. Kerala's rich cultural heritage, traditional dance forms like Kathakali, and delectable cuisine add to the charm of this paradise on Earth.

Himachal - The Abode of the Himalayas

Welcome to the majestic land of Himachal, nestled in the lap of the Himalayas. Experience the thrill of adventure in Manali, with activities like paragliding and river rafting. Delight in the scenic beauty of Shimla, where the toy train ride and snowfall in winter create a magical ambiance. Witness the spiritual aura of Dharamshala, the home of the Dalai Lama, and explore the ancient monasteries. For a serene retreat, visit the mesmerizing hills of Dalhousie and Khajjiar, often referred to as "Mini Switzerland." Himachal's diverse landscapes and warm hospitality make it a perfect destination for every traveler.

Shimla - A Hillside Wonderland

Begin your hillside adventure from Chandigarh to the captivating town of Shimla. Experience the thrill of Kufri and the charm of Manali, surrounded by breathtaking mountains.

Eastern Delights - Unveiling the Beauty of the Eas

Discover the mesmerizing beauty of the East with our carefully curated travel package. From the cultural hub of Guwahati to the tranquil landscapes of Tawang, immerse yourself in the enchanting wonders of the region.

Nainital - Serenity Amidst Nature

Embark on a journey from Delhi to Jim Corbett, where the wilderness will mesmerize you. Discover the natural beauty of Nainital, where sparkling lakes and lush hills await.
