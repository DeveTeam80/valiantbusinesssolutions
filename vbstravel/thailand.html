  • Day 1

    Arrival

    Arrival in Bangkok. Travel to Pattaya. In the evening, enjoy the spectacular Alcazar show. Overnight stay in Pattaya.

  • Day 2

    Pattaya

    Enjoy a speed boat ride, parasailing and swimming in Pattaya's crystal-clear blue waters at Coral Island. Visit Nong Nooch Village for Thai Cultural show and Elephant show. Visit to Tropical Garden, Orchid Garden and Pottery Garden. Overnight stay in Pattaya.

  • Day 3

    Pattaya to Bangkok

    In the morning, travel to Bangkok. Visit the Gems Gallery on the way. The Golden Buddha is the first stop on a city tour, followed by the Principal Buddha and the Reclining Buddha. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

  • Day 4

    Bangkok

    Today enjoy the day at Safari world and visit Marine Park. Explore wildlife like Giraffes, Zebras, Tigers, Lions and many more at Safari Park. Evening is free for shopping. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

  • Day 5

    Departure

    Check out from the hotel and drop at airport to board return flight.
